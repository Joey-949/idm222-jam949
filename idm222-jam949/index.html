About Me

Joseph Anthony McQuillan

My name is Joey McQuillan, and I am a student from Drexel University studying Interactive Digital Media. over the past two years in Drexel, I have been creating and recreating websites, making varying subjects of online content. I want to make products that make peoples lives better.

I enjoy being in the zone for work, with hours of the day quickly speed by, while I make the projects that I care about. If I have the chance to take a break from schoolwork, I love playing fighting games with others, reading books I haven't gotten to, work out, and draw on my sketchbook.
